Statistics for Social Sciences

What is Statistics?

Statistics is a tool which is used by almost everybody practicing science and social studiesat various levels. According to the students at UG and PG levels, choosing statistical tools and conducting necessary testson their data available is the biggest challenge they face. Blindly inserting data for random popular statistical tests like 'T test' and 'F Test' estimates and values and hence make ones's study reliable and vague conclusions are drawn based on that. The proposed idea of a website for Social Science Statistics aims to help people with no statistical background, choose and conduct right statistical tests for not only to guide them with their data but also provide guidelines with the help of examples to for the better the understanding of the mathematics behind the values.
