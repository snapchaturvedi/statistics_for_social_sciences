• Level of Measurement of the Data
    • Nominal
      • One Nominal Variable
        • Chi - Square Test for Goodness of Fit
      • More than One Nominal Variable
        • Chi - Square Test for Independence
    • Ordinal
      • Relationship between the Variables
        • Spreaman's Rho
      • Differences between Populations
        • Independent Measures
          • Mann - Whitney U-test
        • Repeated Measures
          • Wilcoxon Signed - Rank test
    • Scale/Ratio
      • Relationship between the Variables
        • Pearson's R
      • Differences between Populations
        • Single Sample
          • Standard Deviation is known
            • Z - Test
          • Standard Deviation is unknown
            • T - Test
        • More than One Sample
          • Independent Measures
            • Two Samples
              • T - Test for Two Independent Sample Means
            • More than Two Samples
              • One Independent Variable
                • One Way ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)
              • More than One Independent Variables
                • Two Way ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)
          • Repeated Measures
            • Two Treamtment Conditions
              • T- Test
            • More than Two Treamtment Conditions
              • One Way Repeated Measures of ANOVA(Analysis of Variance)
