This wizard will ask you a few questions, and then based on your answers, will recommend a statistics test.

Please note that this wizard is designed to select between statistics tests that you would commonly find being used in the context of undergraduate studies in the social and behavioral sciences.

NOTE: If you're a professional researcher, working on your thesis, you should really consult a statistician primarily.
